About The Vibe Check Project

We believe that stronger relationships start with intentional daily practice. Learn about our mission and the team behind this vision.

Our Story

Born from the recognition that great relationships require more than love—they need consistent, intentional practice.

How We Started

The Vibe Check Project began with a simple observation: most of us know what makes relationships work, but we struggle to consistently apply those principles in our daily lives. We found ourselves and our friends having the same conversations about communication breakdowns, missed connections, and the difficulty of building lasting relationship habits.

We realized that while there's no shortage of relationship advice out there, there was a gap in helping people develop the actual skills through structured, daily practice. Just like physical fitness, emotional intelligence and communication skills require regular exercise to grow and strengthen.

Our Vision

We envision a world where people approach relationship building with the same intentionality and consistency they bring to their careers, fitness, or personal hobbies. A world where the skills for deeper connection, better communication, and emotional intelligence are accessible to everyone through structured, supportive practice.

Our Approach

Rather than overwhelming users with complex psychology or lengthy therapy sessions, we focus on bite-sized daily challenges that build real-world relationship skills. We combine the science of habit formation with practical relationship wisdom to create a system that anyone can follow.

Mission & Values

1

Accessibility

We believe relationship skills shouldn't be exclusive to those who can afford expensive therapy or have hours to spend reading self-help books. Our approach makes relationship growth accessible to everyone, regardless of their schedule or budget.

2

Consistency Over Perfection

Small, daily actions compound over time to create meaningful change. We focus on building sustainable habits rather than expecting overnight transformations or perfect execution.

3

Community Support

Growth happens best in community. We create safe, anonymous spaces where people can share their journey, learn from others' experiences, and find encouragement during the challenging moments.
