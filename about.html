About The Vibe Check Project

A free tool for sending the right words to the right person at exactly the right time.

Our Story

It started with a simple problem: knowing someone needs support but not knowing what to say.

How We Started

Most of us have been there — a friend goes quiet, someone you love is going through a breakup, a coworker just got some hard news. You want to reach out. You want to say something real. But the blank text message cursor just blinks at you, and you end up sending nothing.

The Vibe Check Project was built for that exact moment. A place where you can pick a card that says what you're feeling, write a short note, and send it — free, no account needed, no friction. Because emotional support shouldn't require a subscription or a perfect first draft.

What We Build

Our core product is simple: a free affirmation card sender. Pick a design, write a message, share a link. The recipient gets a beautiful card they can open anywhere, on any device.

For people who want more, we offer a one-time Premium Unlock ($4.99) that gives you access to 10+ premium card design themes (with more added constantly) and all relaxing sounds directly in your browser. Pay once, keep it forever.

Our Approach

We write every affirmation and every blog post ourselves, grounded in real emotional situations — not generic wellness platitudes. If you've ever searched "what to text someone having a panic attack" at 2am and found nothing useful, that's exactly who we're building for.

The site is free. Always will be. The premium option exists so we can keep the lights on and keep building. Every Premium Unlock directly supports new card designs, new affirmations, and new features.

What We're About

1

Zero Friction Kindness

Sending support shouldn't require an account, a credit card, or 20 minutes. The core experience is free, fast, and works on any device. If someone needs a card at 11pm, we're here.

2

Real Words for Real Situations

No filler affirmations. Every card, every email, every blog post is written for a specific human situation — because "you've got this!" means nothing when someone is actually struggling.

3

Transparent & Community Supported

We never sell your data. We're a small independent project supported by the people who find value in what we make, along with select affiliate links and minimal ads to keep the core experience free.
