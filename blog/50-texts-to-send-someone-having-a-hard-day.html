50 Texts for a Hard Day

Exactly what to say through text when someone you care about is having a terrible, no good, very bad day.

When someone you love is going through a rough patch, it’s easy to feel helpless. You want to fix it, but often, the most powerful thing you can do is simply show up. A thoughtful text message doesn't solve the problem, but it serves as a vital anchor, reminding them that they aren't alone in the storm.

A Quick Tip for Sending Support: Don't feel pressured to have the "perfect" advice. Most of the time, people just need validation and presence. If they're overwhelmed, try adding "No need to reply" to your message—it removes the social obligation of responding when they might not have the energy.
