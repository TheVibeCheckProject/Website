Frequently Asked Questions

Is sending a card really free?

Yes. Sending a card costs nothing. No account required, no credit card, no catch. The free experience includes all the standard card designs and a personal message. Premium designs are optional.

Does the recipient need an account to view their card?

Nope. You send them a link. They click it, they see their card. That's it. No app download, no sign-up, no friction for them.

What is the Premium Unlock?

A one-time $4.99 purchase that unlocks 10+ premium card design themes (with more added constantly) — Birthday, Grief Support, Hype-Up, Love, Anxiety Relief, Friendship, Self-Love, Congratulations, Just Because, and "I See You" — plus all relaxing sounds. Yours forever, right in your browser.

I bought something but have an issue — what do I do?

Email us at WeCare@TheVibeCheckProject.com and we'll sort it out.

Can I unsubscribe from the email list?

Yes — every email we send has an unsubscribe link at the bottom. One click and you're off. We use MailerLite and they handle opt-outs immediately.
