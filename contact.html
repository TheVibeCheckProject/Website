Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about The Vibe Check Project.

How do I get started?

Simply sign up through our app or website form, and you'll begin receiving daily relationship challenges tailored to your growth areas. Start with small, manageable steps and build momentum over time.

Is this therapy or counseling?

No, The Vibe Check Project is not a replacement for professional therapy or counseling. We provide structured daily practices and community support, but recommend consulting qualified professionals for serious relationship issues or mental health concerns.

How much does it cost?

We're currently in development and testing phase. Pricing details will be announced as we get closer to our full launch. Early supporters may receive special access or pricing.
