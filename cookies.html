Cookie Policy

Learn about how we use cookies and tracking technologies to improve your experience.

Last Updated: January 2025

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device when you visit our website. They help us provide you with a better experience by remembering your preferences and understanding how you use our site.

2. How We Use Cookies

We use cookies for the following purposes:

Essential Cookies

  • Authentication and login status
  • Security and fraud prevention
  • Basic website functionality
  • Remembering your preferences

Analytics Cookies

  • Understanding how users navigate our site
  • Measuring page performance and load times
  • Identifying popular content and features
  • Improving user experience

Functional Cookies

  • Remembering your language preferences
  • Storing your progress through our challenges
  • Customizing content based on your interests
  • Enabling interactive features

3. Third-Party Cookies

We may use third-party services that set their own cookies, including:

  • Google Analytics: For website usage analytics
  • Font Services: Google Fonts may set cookies for font delivery
  • Social Media: If integrated, social platforms may set cookies

4. Managing Cookies

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to:

  • View which cookies are stored on your device
  • Delete cookies individually or all at once
  • Block cookies from specific websites
  • Block all cookies entirely

Note: Disabling cookies may affect the functionality of our website and your user experience.

5. Cookie Categories

Session Cookies

These are temporary cookies that are deleted when you close your browser. They help maintain your session while navigating our site.

Persistent Cookies

These cookies remain on your device for a set period or until you delete them. They remember your preferences across multiple visits.

6. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or applicable laws. We will notify you of any significant changes by updating the "Last Updated" date.
