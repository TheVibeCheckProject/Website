Cookie Policy

Learn about how we use cookies and tracking technologies to improve your experience.

Last Updated: February 2025

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit a website. Some cookies are set by the site itself; others are set by third-party services the site uses.

2. Our Approach: Minimal Tracking

The Vibe Check Project is designed to work with minimal cookies. We do not use advertising cookies, behavioral tracking cookies, or any cross-site tracking technology.

We use Microsoft Clarity for website analytics. Clarity provides heatmaps and session recordings without advertising cookies or cross-site tracking. It does not set persistent advertising cookies on your device.

3. Cookies That May Be Set

Google Fonts

We load fonts from Google Fonts. Google may log the font request (your IP address and the font requested) as part of normal web server logging. This is a one-time request per page load and does not track your behavior across sites.

MailerLite (Email Subscribers Only)

If you click a link in one of our emails, MailerLite may set a cookie to track whether you opened or clicked an email. This only applies if you are subscribed to our email list. You can unsubscribe at any time using the link in any email.

Ko-fi & Gumroad (Paying Members Only)

If you visit Ko-fi or Gumroad to complete a purchase, those platforms set their own cookies on their domains. We have no control over these cookies. See their respective privacy policies for details.

localStorage (Not Cookies)

We use browser localStorage (not cookies) to remember small pieces of information on your device, such as whether you've dismissed a banner. This data never leaves your browser and is not shared with anyone.

4. What We Don't Use

  • Google Analytics — we use Microsoft Clarity instead
  • Facebook Pixel or any Meta tracking
  • Advertising or retargeting cookies
  • Login session cookies (no accounts on this site)

5. Managing Cookies

You can view and delete cookies at any time through your browser settings. Since we use Microsoft Clarity (no advertising cookies), blocking cookies will not affect your experience on our site.

6. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy when our practices change. Updates will be reflected by the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page.
