# Pinterest Strategy for The Vibe Check Project

Pinterest is a visual search engine, not a social network. It is the #1 traffic driver for blogs in the mental health, relationships, and self-care niches.

The strategy here is simple: **Turn your copyable messages into highly readable, aesthetically pleasing Pins that people want to save for later.**

## Core Strategy

1. **Volume over Perfection:** You need to pin 2-5 times a day.
2. **Batch Creation:** Use the `pinterest-pin-generator.html` tool to take dozens of screenshots of different messages in 15 minutes.
3. **Keyword Rich Descriptions:** Pinterest relies on text descriptors to know who to show your pins to. Use the exact titles of your blog posts as the pin titles.
4. **Link Directly:** Every pin must link back to the exact blog post or listicle where that message is found.

## Pin Design Rules

- **Aspect Ratio:** Always 1000px wide by 1500px tall (2:3 ratio). The generator tool handles this perfectly.
- **Background:** High contrast. Dark background with bright gradient text, or bright colored cards with dark text.
- **Font:** Massive, readable fonts. 80% of Pinterest users are on mobile. If they can't read the text while scrolling fast, they won't stop.
- **Branding:** Always include `thevibecheckproject.com` at the bottom of the pin so it can't be stolen without crediting you.

## The Workflow

1. Open `pinterest-pin-generator.html` in your browser.
2. Open `data/messages.json` or one of your listicles (e.g., 100 Encouraging Messages).
3. Paste a compelling text message into the generator.
4. Type a hook for the header (e.g., "Text this to a friend having a panic attack:").
5. Screenshot the generator preview area. (On Mac: `Cmd + Shift + 4`, On Windows: `Windows Key + Shift + S`).
6. Upload to Pinterest with the title of the blog post, a 2-sentence description, and the URL to the exact post.

## Recommended Boards to Create

Create these boards on your Pinterest profile to categorize your pins:
- Mental Health Reminders
- What to Text Someone
- Grief & Loss Support
- Breakup Advice & Healing
- Daily Affirmations & Quotes
- Friendship & Support
